Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

What we collect

THERMOGYS is a simple B2B landing site with a contact form. We do not collect any personal data except the information you voluntarily submit when you ask for information or request a reply.

The contact form only collects the details needed to answer your message, such as your name, email address, company name, and message.

No cookies and no tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, advertising trackers, or similar tracking technologies on this site.

How we use your message

Messages submitted through the contact form are used only to reply to your inquiry and to handle business requests. We do not use this information for marketing, profiling, or advertising.

Data retention

We keep contact messages only as long as needed to answer your request and to maintain a basic record of the exchange. If you prefer that we delete your message, please contact us.

Third parties

We do not share contact form submissions with third parties unless required by law. If a message is forwarded to a partner or subcontractor to answer your request, we only share the minimum information needed to handle it.
